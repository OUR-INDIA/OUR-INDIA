Harmandir Sahib Golden Temple
Of Punjab.

  • Located in the heart of Amritsar, the Golden Temple is the most admired gurudwara in the country.
  • The Golden Temple namely Sri Harmandir Sahib serves as an epitome of glory, harmony and equality. Sri Harmandir Sahib played an integral role in the rich history of Punjab which makes this religious heritage the holiest pilgrimage site for the Sikhs.
  • Golden Temple is Said To Be Golden temple As It Is Fully Covered With Gold Foil.
